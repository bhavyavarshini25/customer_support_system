COMPANY POLICY DOCUMENT - ABC Technologies

1. REFUND POLICY
- Refunds are available within 30 days of purchase for annual subscriptions.
- Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable after the billing cycle starts.
- Refund requests must be approved by a billing supervisor.
- Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days.

2. SUBSCRIPTION CANCELLATION POLICY
- Customers can cancel anytime. Service continues until end of billing period.
- Cancellation of annual plans requires supervisor approval to issue prorated refund.
- Account data is retained for 90 days after cancellation.

3. ACCOUNT CLOSURE POLICY
- Account closure is permanent and requires supervisor approval.
- All data is deleted after 90-day retention window.
- Customers must settle any outstanding invoices before closure.

4. COMPENSATION POLICY
- Compensation (credits, discounts) for service disruptions requires management approval.
- SLA violations of >4 hours qualify for service credits.
- Compensation up to 20% of monthly bill can be approved by billing supervisor.

5. ESCALATION POLICY
- Unresolved issues after 48 hours escalate to senior support.
- Critical data loss or security incidents escalate immediately to management.

6. SUPPORT HOURS
- Standard support: Monday–Friday, 9 AM–6 PM (local time).
- Emergency support: 24/7 for critical outages.
